How it Works?

  • 1
    STEP

    UPLOAD

    Select your PDF file by clicking the upload area or dragging and dropping the file

  • 2
    STEP

    SUMMARY LANGUAGE

    Choose your preferred summary language from the dropdown menu.

  • 3
    STEP

    SUMMARY LENGTH

    Select the summary length: Short, Medium, or Full.

  • 4
    STEP

    SUMMARIZE

    Click the Summarize button.

    Wait a few seconds while your summary is generated.

  • 5
    STEP

    COPY/DOWLOAD

    Your summary will appear below. You can copy it or download it as a text file.

Our service allows up to 5 summary requests per day for the entire website, as limited by our AI service provider’s plan. This means all users share this daily quota. Once the limit is reached, no more summaries can be generated until the next day.

If the daily limit is reached, you’ll see a message : "Today’s summary limit has been reached, New summaries will be available tomorrow." Summarization will be unavailable until the daily quota resets.

Our summarizer supports multiple languages, including Bengali, English, Gujarati, Hindi, Kannada, Kashmiri, Marathi, Oriya, Punjabi, and Urdu. Simply select your preferred language from the dropdown before summarizing your PDF.
